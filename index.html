What is Garry's Mod

Garry's Mod (GMod) is a sandbox game where players can manipulate objects, build structures, and create custom content. It started as a mod for Half-Life 2 and grew into its own game with a large modding community. Players have endless freedom to experiment and play in diverse ways, making it popular for creativity and social interaction.

About Project Zenin

Welcome to Project Zenin, your portal to custom Halo roleplaying adventures set in the year 2525 and beyond! Join the ranks as a Marine, ODST or Spartan, and immerse yourself in a universe where every decision shapes the course of history. As we journey forward in time, explore new frontiers and face fresh challenges, all within our dynamic and evolving server. Stay tuned as we venture beyond Garry's Mod to bring you even more thrilling entertainment experiences in the future. Your epic saga begins here at Project Zenin!
